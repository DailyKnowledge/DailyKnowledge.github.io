Natural selection needs variation, inheritance, and differential survival

Evolution by natural selection occurs when traits vary, are inherited, and affect reproductive success.

Takeaway

Selection is not a plan; it is a filter acting on existing variation.

What I learned

Natural selection requires three ingredients: variation among individuals, inheritance of some traits, and differences in survival or reproduction connected to those traits.

Why it matters

This helps avoid thinking of evolution as intentional progress. Selection works with what already exists and changes populations over generations.

A useful framing

Ask what varies, what is inherited, and what changes the odds of leaving descendants.
